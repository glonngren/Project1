Introduction

My name is George Lonngren, I am 27 years old and I live in the West suburbs of Chicago in a town called Lombard, Illinois. I was born in raised in the West Suburbs and graduated High School from Willowbrook HS in Villa Park, IL. Through out High School, I had kept a promise to myself that I would serve in the military after witnessing the attacks on 9/11 in New York, Pennsylvania and Washington D.C. I graduated high school early to join the United States Marine Corps.

In 2009 I enlisted as a Communications Equipment Repairer. I've fixed almost any type of communications gear that plugs into a wall, from XBOX 360s to rotary telephones. After serving five years in the Marines, I was honorably discharged and became a field engineer in the oil fields of Oklahoma. I was personally involved in the construction of over one-hundred wells. After oil prices dropped, I was laid off and made the decision to go to college, and thats where I am today.
